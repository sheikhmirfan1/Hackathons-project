    Emergency Services

    Women protection

    The nationwide support hotline “Violence Against Women" simplifies access to the support system for the persons concerned. It is primarily directed at women who have experienced violence. But also relatives, acquaintances, and professionals can make use of the free counselling service. The support hotline is also open to trans persons who are affected by violence. Available Day and Night – Anonymous and Free of Charge The support hotline can be reached day and night under 08000 116 016 – free of charge, and anonymous if desired. Moreover, the website www.hilfetelefon.de offers online counselling via e-mail or chat. The online counselling service is also available from outside of Germany, whereas the hotline is only available in Germany.
